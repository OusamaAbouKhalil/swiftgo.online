A Quick Guide to Swift Go

Here’s how the Swift Go app connects riders, drivers, and services on demand, step by step:

1. Getting Started

Open the Swift Go app, enter your destination in the “Where to?” box, review ride options, select your preference, and confirm your pickup location.

2. Matching Rider and Driver

A nearby driver accepts your request. You’ll get a notification as they approach your location.

3. Picking Up

The driver and you verify each other’s names and destination. Then, the ride begins.

4. Taking the Trip

The app provides turn-by-turn directions to guide the driver to your destination.

5. Leaving Ratings and Reviews

After the trip, you and the driver can rate each other from 1 to 5 stars. You can also tip directly in the app and leave compliments for your driver.
